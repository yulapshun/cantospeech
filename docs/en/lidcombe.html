Stuttering Program

Lidcombe Program

Parents are trained by a speech-language pathologist to deliver specific verbal feedback in everyday situations to reinforce fluent speech and gently acknowledge stuttering.
  • Research-based
    The Program was developed by the Australian Stuttering Research Centre
  • Uses real-life conversations
  • During normal daily activities
  • Parent-led. clinician-supervised and child-friendly
