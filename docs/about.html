About
關於

I’m Amanda, a registered Speech-Language Pathologist with the College of Audiologists and Speech-Language Pathologists of Ontario (CASLPO). I have experience supporting children with language disorders, speech sound difficulties, learning challenges, and Autism Spectrum Disorder.

I provide speech and language services (regulated service) to children and their families, both in-home and online.

I also offer personalized online Cantonese lessons (non-regulated service) for learners of all ages, helping them build confidence, enjoy the language, and grow at their own pace.

Hello, 我叫Amanda。我曾於香港社福機構任言語治療師，現於加拿大以獨立言語治療師 (Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP, Reg. CASLPO) 形式執業，提供上門及視像言語治療服務（受監管服務)。同時，我亦提供線上廣東話課程（非受監管服務），適合所有有意學習廣東話的人士。
