Services 服務內容

Speech Therapy Services (age 2 to 17)

  1. Fill in an online intake form – to share your goals, learning preferences, and background. https://forms.gle/L2vvTgKtjAzdy95cA

  2. 60-minute assessment session (includes a 10-minute debrief) – to conduct an assessment for different areas (such as language comprehension, expressive language, speech sounds, social communication, and higher-order thinking), and then we review the assessment results together and provide tailored recommendations.

    *Assessment summary and goals sheet can be included upon request.

  3. 60-minute therapy sessions and parent coaching (includes a 10-minute debrief) – based on your child’s needs, to support growth in communication, confidence, and language skills.

    Services are available in English and Cantonese (I can communicate with parents through Mandarin as well). Sessions are available online or via home visits.

    If you have concerns about your child’s language and communication development, you are welcome to contact me for a free 10-minute initial consultation.

言語評估和治療 (2歲-17歲)

  1. 填寫線上諮詢表（評估前）—分享孩子的背景資料、喜好和學習目標。https://forms.gle/L2vvTgKtjAzdy95cA

  2. 60分鐘評估 （包括10分鐘家長咨詢）—與孩子進行測試（包括：語言理解、語言表達、咬字發音、社交溝通及高階思考等等）。完成評估後，我會解釋評估結果，進行家長咨詢，並一同訂立治療方案。

    *如有需要，可提供評估摘要及學習目標表。

  3. 60分鐘治療課程與家長指導（包括10分鐘家長咨詢）—我會按孩子的需要、能力和興趣設計治療課程，同時提供家長指導，協助家長學習語言促進技巧，在日常生活中幫助孩子提升語言與溝通能力。

    評估及治療服務以英語或廣東話進行（我亦能以普通話跟家長溝通）。可視像或上門。

    若你對孩子的語言和溝通能力發展有疑問，歡迎聯絡我進行初步10分鐘免費諮詢。

Online Cantonese Class (for all ages)

  1. Fill in an online intake form – to share your goals, learning preferences, and background. https://forms.gle/yxB4jq5rDB6FMMKh8

  2. 45-minute or 60-minute lessons(includes a 5-minute debrief; 1:1/small group setting) – engaging, interactive activities tailored to your needs, including theme-based activities/games,songs, stories, functional speech, and more.

    *Reading and writing – can be included upon request to support literacy development in Chinese.

    Services are available in Cantonese (supplement in English and Mandarin). For more details about the courses, feel free to get in touch.

線上廣東話課程（適合各年齡層）

  1. 填寫線上報名表—分享孩子的背景資料、喜好和學習目標。https://forms.gle/yxB4jq5rDB6FMMKh8

  2. 45 或60分鐘課程（包括5分鐘家長咨詢;一對一／小組教學）—包含主題活動/遊戲、兒歌/歌曲、故事、日常對話等互動。

    *認字/閱讀及書寫 – 可按需要融入課堂中。

    課程以廣東話為主（可輔以英語及普通話）。若有意了解課程詳情，歡迎查詢。
