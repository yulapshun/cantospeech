About 關於

I’m Amanda, a committed Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP) with experience supporting children with language disorders, speech sound difficulties, learning challenges, and Autism Spectrum Disorder. I provide speech and language service to children and their families, both in-home and online. I also offer personalized online Cantonese lessons for learners of all ages, helping them build confidence, enjoy the language, and grow at their own pace.

Hello, 我叫Amanda. 我曾於香港社福機構任言語治療師，現於加拿大任言語病理學家 (Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP))，同時以獨立言語病理學家形式執業，提供上門及視像言語治療服務。同時，我亦提供線上廣東話課程，適合所有有意學習廣東話的人士。
