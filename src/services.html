Speech Therapy Services
(age 2 to 17)

  1. 60-minute assessment session (includes a 10-minute debrief)

    • To conduct an assessment for language comprehension, expressive language, speech sounds, social communication, and higher-order thinking
    • To review the assessment results and provide tailored recommendations
    • Assessment summary and goals sheet can be included upon request

  2. 60-minute therapy sessions and parent coaching (includes a 10-minute debrief)

Services are available in English and Cantonese (I can communicate with parents through Mandarin as well). Sessions are available online or via home visits.

If you have concerns about your child’s language and communication development, you are welcome to contact me for a free 10-minute initial consultation.

言語評估和治療
（2歲-17歲）

  1. 60分鐘評估（包括10分鐘家長咨詢）

    • 與孩子進行評估（包括：語言理解、語言表達、咬字發音、社交溝通及高階思考等等）
    • 完成評估後，我會解釋評估結果，並與家長一同訂立治療方案
    • 如有需要，可提供評估摘要及學習目標表

  2. 60分鐘治療課程與家長指導（包括10分鐘家長咨詢）

評估及治療服務以英語或廣東話進行（我亦能以普通話跟家長溝通）。可視像或上門。

若你對孩子的語言/溝通能力發展有疑問，歡迎聯絡我作10分鐘免費諮詢。
